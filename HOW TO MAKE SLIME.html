The First Thing You Want To Do Is To Spark Your Creativity!

To start off let me explain slime. Slime is a stress reliever that is blown up all over the internet.

ACTIVATOR: eyecontact solution and baking soda (DYE IS OPTIONAL)

INGREDIENTS

    Regular

    1. glue
    2. baking soda
    3. eyecontact solution

    Cloud Creme

    1. fake snow
    2. white glue
    3. activator
    4. water

    Butter Slime

    1. daiso clay
    2. white glue
    3. activator

    Icy Slime

    1. clear glue
    2. activator
    3. plastic fake snow

    Clear Slime

    1. clear glue
    2. activator

    first you going to want to add your glue into your bowl, and add the dye

    Then you have to add in a pinch of baking soda

    then gradually add you eye contact solution until the glue mixture doesn't stick to the sides
